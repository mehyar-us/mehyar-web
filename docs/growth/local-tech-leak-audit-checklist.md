## 10-point leak check
1. Mobile first impression: loads fast enough, credible, no broken hero/CTA.
2. One-tap action: phone, booking, or contact CTA is visible above the fold on mobile.
3. Contact path works: forms submit correctly; no mailto-only dead end unless intentional.
4. After-hours path: visitor knows what happens if nobody answers.
5. Missed-call recovery: owner has a way to capture, tag, and follow up with missed inquiries.
6. Booking friction: service/menu, location, hours, and next step are easy to find.
7. Review signal: public reviews mention missed calls, scheduling friction, slow response, or confusion.
8. Source tracking: owner can identify whether leads came from Google, social, referrals, or ads.
9. CRM/follow-up: inquiries become trackable records with owner reminders, not just inbox clutter.
10. Compliance basics: opt-out, consent, privacy language, and sensitive-data boundaries are appropriate for the vertical.

## Operating guardrails
- Manual review only; no scraping blast or automated outreach.
- Record source URL and pain signal before any contact.
- Check suppression/opt-out before every touch.
- For clinics, dental, med spa, legal, financial, or other sensitive verticals: discuss operations only; do not request PHI, case details, private client/customer data, or claim outcomes.
